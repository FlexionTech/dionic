Meet Dr Di Ashworth – Founder of DIONIC

Dr Di Ashworth is the founder of DIONIC and a dedicated Social Worker with a diverse and rich background in disability support, education, counselling, and research. Di brings a holistic and deeply personal perspective to her work, shaped by both professional expertise and lived experience.

Her journey includes firsthand involvement with the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) as a Planner, where she had gained valuable insights into the support landscape. She have also contributed my expertise at Vision Australia (VA), coordinating the Quality Living program, and at Women’s Information Referral Exchange (WIRE) as a Counsellor.

Driven by a passion for understanding the human experience, my PhD explored young people's identity development. Dr Di Ashworth's commitment to innovation is further highlighted by participating as the first research participant in the Australian Bionic Eye project.

Today, her combined professional skills with personal experience of vision loss to ensure digital spaces are accessible to all, testing web content with the JAWS screen reader. Dr Di Ashworth vision for DIONIC is rooted in working alongside individuals living with vision loss, fostering strength and resilience.
