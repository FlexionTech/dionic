Profile

Hello! I'm Dr. Di Ashworth, the heart and founder of DIONIC. As a Social Worker with a rich and varied career, I bring a unique depth of understanding to my work. My experience spans supporting people with disabilities, guiding students as a lecturer, and providing crucial counseling services.

My journey includes firsthand involvement with the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) as a Planner, where I gained valuable insights into the support landscape. I’ve also contributed my expertise at Vision Australia (VA), coordinating the Quality Living program, and at Women’s Information Referral Exchange (WIRE) as a Counsellor.

Driven by a passion for understanding the human experience, my PhD explored young people's identity development. My commitment to innovation is further highlighted by participating as the first research participant in the Australian Bionic Eye project.

Today, I combine professional skills with personal experience of vision loss to ensure digital spaces are accessible to all, testing web content with the JAWS screen reader. My vision for DIONIC is rooted in working alongside individuals living with vision loss, fostering strength and resilience.
